Digital cameras used to capture portraits shall produce images where the vertical and horizontal pixel density is the same.
Live captured portraits of a subject
- Shall be captured in one of the following formats: PNG, JPEG, JPEG2000, RAW formats supported by the camera, lossless formats should be preferred;
- Should be captured at a minimum dimension of 1200 pixels width x 1600 pixels height (cropped image);
- Shall be captured in colour.
